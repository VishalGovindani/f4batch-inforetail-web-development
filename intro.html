Information about Covid-19

Introduction

  • Coronavirus disease 2019 or COVID-19 is a respiratory illness that can spread from person to person.
  • It is defined as illness caused by a severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS- CoV-2; formerly called 2019-nCoV)
  • It was first identified amid an out of respiratory illness cases in Wuhan City, Hubie Province, China.
  • Initial case-patients reported visiting a large seafood and live animal market in Wuhan.
  • Coronaviruses have a crownlike appearance under the microscope.
  • The COVID-19 virus spreads primarily through droplets of saliva or discharge from the nose when an infected person coughs or sneezes.
  • It was initially reported to the World Health Organization (WHO) on December 31, 2019. On January 30, 2020, the WHO declared the COVID-19 a GLOBAL HEALTH EMERGENCY.
